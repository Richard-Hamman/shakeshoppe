about us

established in 2021

The Shake Shoppe is a family-run business that specializes in milkshakes and Ice creams. We delight in bringing you the most delicious artisan milkshakes and sweet treats at amazing prices.

We recently branched out into snacks and salty treats by adding deep fried potato twisters and crispy hot chips to our menu.

You can usually find us right on the beach at Granny’s Pool Beach on Compensation Beach Rd in Salt Rock, Ballito. To enquire about our catering services for parties.
